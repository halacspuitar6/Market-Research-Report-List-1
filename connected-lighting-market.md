<p><p>The Connected Lighting market encompasses various types, including Network Control and Power-over-Ethernet (PoE). Network Control utilizes advanced protocols to enable centralized management of lighting systems, allowing for enhanced energy efficiency, remote monitoring, and automated adjustments based on occupancy or daylight availability. Meanwhile, Power-over-Ethernet integrates data and power transmission over standard Ethernet cables, facilitating simpler installations and reducing wiring complexity. Both technologies contribute to smarter, more responsive lighting solutions in commercial, industrial, and residential settings.</p></p>
