<p><p>Industrial 5G networks comprise two key market types: Sub-6 GHz and mmWave. Sub-6 GHz operates at lower frequencies, offering broad coverage and reliable penetration in industrial environments, making it ideal for use in factories and warehouses. Conversely, mmWave utilizes higher frequencies, enabling significantly faster data rates and lower latency, suitable for high-demand applications like real-time analytics and automation. Both types support enhanced connectivity and efficiency, driving the digital transformation of industries.</p></p>
